An AI that experiences your product like a real user would — then tells you, bluntly, what bugs them and what they miss.

most users never tell you why they left. we do. 🍖

the problem

your product's first impression is a black box.

You ship. People land. Most get confused, shrug, and leave — silently. No bug report, no feedback, no second chance. You're optimizing in the dark.

"works on my machine" ≠ "makes sense to a stranger"

what it is

a stand-in user, on demand.

Point userroast at a repo, landing page, or app. It reads it the way a first-time visitor would — no insider context — and reacts honestly. You get the reaction you'd normally only get from a churned user who never wrote back.

how it works

01

02
