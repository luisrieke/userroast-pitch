01 · problem + customer

most users leave without a word.

A solo founder ships a dev tool or SaaS. First-time visitors land, get confused in the first 30 seconds, and bounce — no bug report, no feedback, no second chance. Analytics show the drop-off; never the reason.

who has it

early-stage founders & PMs — pre-seed to seed dev-tool, SaaS and open-source teams of 1–20 people shipping something new.

the pain

they can't see why first-run users churn. the people who would tell them already left, silently.

what they do today

beg friends, post on HN / Reddit, or pay $100+/session for recruited user tests that take a week to schedule.

🍖 the honest version

"works on my machine" ≠ "makes sense to a stranger."
